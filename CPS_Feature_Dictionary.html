CPS Enrollment Forecasting — Feature Dictionary

What every model input means, how it is built, and why it is leak-free · one row per school × grade × school year

The goal. Predict next year's enrollment for each school & grade. Two models split the work: a GBT specialist for grades 1–8 & 10–12, and an XGBoost specialist for the entry grades K & 9.

The golden rule (no leakage). Every feature uses only information available before the year being predicted — last year, two years ago, or a survey from a prior year. Nothing from the target year is ever used.

Reading the fill rate. It is the share of rows that have a real value. *_AVG_3YR features are ~100% because they use whatever history exists (even just one year). *_2YR_AGO and *_TREND need a second year of history, so they sit near 84–89%. Anything still missing is filled with the training-set median for the GBT, and kept as-is for XGBoost (which handles blanks natively).
